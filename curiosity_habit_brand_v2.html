— Logo Concept —

The icon combines a compass — symbolizing exploration, direction and curiosity about the world — with an eye at the center, representing knowledge, observation and the act of truly seeing. The amber north needle points upward, suggesting growth and aspiration. The teal tones evoke depth, mystery and the vast unknown. Together they say: there is always a story behind everything — explore it.
