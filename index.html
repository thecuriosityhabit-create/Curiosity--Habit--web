About The Channel

The Curiosity Habit was created by a lifelong learner who believes the world is far more fascinating than most people realize.

History is stranger than fiction. Nature defies imagination. Ancient mysteries still puzzle the greatest minds. And the future is arriving faster than anyone expected.

There is a story behind everything — and most of those stories were never properly told. Not in school, not in textbooks and not in the headlines. This channel exists to change that.
